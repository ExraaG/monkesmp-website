Server Laws

Is griefing and stealing allowed?

Yes. This is an anarchy/survival experience at its core. Base raiding, stealing, and griefing are fully permitted. Protect your chests and build far away.

What are the bannable offenses?

Hacking (X-Ray, KillAura, etc.), exploiting dupes, DDoS threats, Doxxing, and extreme toxicity/slurs are zero-tolerance. Combat Logging during PvP results in automatic death.

Are alliances allowed?

Yes, teaming is allowed. However, betrayal is also allowed. Trust wisely.

What version of Minecraft?

The server supports the latest versions. We highly recommend using 1.21.11 for the best experience.
